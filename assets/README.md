# Valentine's Special Assets

This directory contains the assets for the Valentine's special celebration.

## Structure

- `video/` - Place your romantic video files here
- `images/` - Place your love story photos here
- `audio/` - Place your romantic music or voice messages here

## Instructions

1. Add your romantic video as `valentine-video.mp4` in the video folder
2. Add your love story photos as `love1.jpg`, `love2.jpg`, `love3.jpg` in the images folder
3. Add romantic music as `love-song.mp3` in the audio folder
4. Update the file paths in the HTML files to match your assets

## Recommended Assets

### Videos
- Romantic montage video
- Personal message video
- Couple's highlights

### Images
- Couple photos
- Memory pictures
- Romantic moments

### Audio
- Love songs
- Personal voice messages
- Romantic music
